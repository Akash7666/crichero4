Maharashtra

Maharashtra has more business than any other one state of India. There are some well-known cave monuments like Ajanta and Ellora. The first oil mill is in Mumbai. Some of the crops grown in Maharashtra are Bajra, Jowar, Rice, Wheat, etc.

Rajasthan

Rajasthan is India's largest state by area (342,239 km2 which scattered in 10.4% of India's total area. It is most famous for its cultural and natural beauty.

Madhyapradesh

Madhya Pradesh is the second largest Indian state by area and the fifth largest state by population with over 72 million residents. It borders the states of Uttar Pradesh to the northeast, Chhattisgarh to the southeast, Maharashtra to the south, Gujarat to the west, and Rajasthan to the northwest.
